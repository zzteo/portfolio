ㅤㅤㅤThis is meant to be an educational VR game that takes you on a journey where you are a sailor subordinated to the admiral Michiel de Ruyter during one of the Anglo-Dutch War. The game takes places on the well-known ‘’zeven provincien’’ ship and you will get to experience what it was like to be a crew member at that time and what were some of the activities they had to do. You will get to find out about his journey to England, how he sank the English fleet and the way back. The game tries to present you the good and the bad side of the beloved admiral, that is why it will also bring to your attention the slavery situation at that time and how Michiel actually reacted and contributed to the war.

ㅤㅤㅤThe game is meant to be played by 11-12 years old primary school students as an auxiliary material after the kids have been familiarized with Michiel de Ruyter by their teacher. ㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤㅤ
