Our client suffers from cerebral palsy and is only able to slightly move his head. He has a wheelchair, and that wheelchair has 2 side supports that act as buttons if pushed. Because there are not many games optimized for his handicap, we wanted to make a game that is easy replayable. It’s a puzzle game where you have to find the way out of a maze, sometimes it requires you to put boxes on their corresponding platform so you can open doors.
